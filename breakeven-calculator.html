üí° Understanding Your Breakeven Analysis

Profit Per Sale: This is your ACTUAL profit after every single cost‚ÄîEtsy fees, shipping, materials, everything except your fixed costs. This is the amount that goes toward paying off your startup costs.

Breakeven Point: Once you sell this many items, you've covered all your startup costs and every sale after this is pure profit (minus variable costs per item).

Revenue Target: This is the total dollars you need to bring in (not profit) to reach breakeven. Track this in your Etsy dashboard.

After Breakeven: Once you hit this milestone, your profit per sale increases dramatically because you're no longer paying down fixed costs!

Realistic Timeline: If you sell 2 items per day, divide your breakeven number by 60 to estimate months to profitability. Adjust your marketing strategy based on this timeline.
