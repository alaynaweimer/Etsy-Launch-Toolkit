üí° Making Smart Pricing Decisions

Profit Margin Matters: A 40%+ profit margin gives you room for growth, unexpected costs, and advertising spend. Below 30% is risky.

Free Shipping Psychology: Studies show free shipping can increase conversion rates by 20-30%, even when the total cost is identical to paid shipping options.

Price Testing: Don't set and forget. Test different prices every 2-3 months. Track views and conversion rates to find your optimal price point.

Volume vs. Margin: Higher prices mean fewer sales but more profit per sale. Lower prices mean more sales but thinner margins. Your ideal strategy depends on production capacity and market demand.

Competitive Analysis: Check similar products every month. If competitors drop prices, you may need to adjust‚Äîor differentiate through better photos/branding to justify premium pricing.
